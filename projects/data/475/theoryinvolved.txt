<h3 style="text-align: center;"><u></u><u></u><u><span style="color: rgb(255, 255, 255);">SOUND SOURCE LOCALIZATION</span></u></h3><p style="text-align: justify;">
	Sound signals travel as longitudinal waves from one point to the other. Two sound detectors placed nearby are expected to receive identical sound signals but with a time delay.
