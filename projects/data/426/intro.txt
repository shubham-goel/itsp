<p>It is an<span style="color: rgb(149, 55, 52);"></span><strong><span style="color: rgb(149, 55, 52);"> Security App</span></strong> designed for people( specially women and children).</p><p>It could be used , when a person is in an insecure area or feeling unsafe and in a need of urgent help.</p><p>It works as follows:-</p><p>If she opens the app and triggers the <span style="color: rgb(79, 129, 189);">launch Button</span> then  a<span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0);"> Voice call is made to the nearby Police Station </span>( could be changed to any of other contact too but default is police station). </p><p>While simultaneously her <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0);">present location address will be sent to the preffered contacts</span>( maximum limit 4 is given)  and the<span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0);"> mobile will acts as a server</span> and would also send its Ip adddress in the message .</p><p>So the person who is receiving the message would know the present location and via <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0);">IP address he can track her position in map  continously(just like real time navigation</span> , for that he would also require the app for traacking or through any other tracking device).</p><p>Except Tracking  all other features are offline which makes it more reliable.</p>
